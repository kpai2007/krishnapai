Solar-Powered Smart Farming With Energy Analytics

Case Study by Krishna Pai V, BSc Physics (First Year), The Cochin College

Project Abstract

This interdisciplinary project integrates IoT technology and Energy Analytics to solve the agricultural crisis in Kerala. By using an ESP32-based system powered by solar energy, we automate irrigation for high-value crops like Pepper, Nutmeg, and Paddy.

Hardware & IoT Stack

ESP32 Microcontroller
Capacitive Moisture Sensors
MPPT Charge Controller
12V DC Pump System
